Fit parameters for equation:

log10 (τ/τo) = (J /To)2(To/T - 1)2

To is the fitted onset temperature in K.

J is the fitted energy scale over kB.

τo is the fitted onset relaxation time in seconds.

ηo is the fitted onset viscosity in Poise.

Σ is the standard deviation of the quadratic form given by (1/(N − n)Σi(log10 τfit,i − log10 τdata,i)2)1/2. N is the number of fitted data points, n = 3 is the number of degrees of freedom for all reported fits, i = {1, N} indexes the fitted points, ΣVFT is the standard deviation for fitting the parameters τoVFT,  A and TK of the VFT form τ = τoVFT exp(A/(T − TK)).

Tm is the melting temperature.

Tg is the glass-transition temperature, i.e., where η = 1013 P or τ = 102 s.

range indicates the range of temperatures for the data is reported in K. For all fits, only data for T < To are fitted.
